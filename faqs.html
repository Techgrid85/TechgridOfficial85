Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

Ordering & Payments

How do I place an order?

You can place an order directly through our website by adding items to your cart and proceeding through checkout. We accept all major credit cards and PayPal.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your payment information. We don't store your credit card details on our servers.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, and Apple Pay. For corporate accounts, we also offer invoice payment options.

Shipping & Delivery

What shipping options do you offer?

We offer standard (3-5 business days), expedited (2 business days), and overnight shipping options. Free standard shipping is available for orders over $1000.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Some restrictions may apply to certain products.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website.

Returns & Warranty

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Items must be in original condition with all packaging. Some exclusions apply for special order items.

Do your products come with a warranty?

Most products come with a 1-year manufacturer's warranty. Extended warranty options are available for purchase at checkout for eligible items.

How do I initiate a return?

Log into your account and visit the "Orders" section to start a return. You'll receive a return authorization and shipping label if applicable. Returns without authorization may be refused.

Product Information

Do you offer technical specifications for your products?

Yes, complete technical specifications are available on each product page under the "Specifications" tab. You can also download PDF spec sheets for most products.

Are your products new or refurbished?

Unless clearly marked as "Refurbished" or "Open Box," all our products are brand new and come with full manufacturer warranties.

Do you sell genuine manufacturer products?

Absolutely. We are an authorized retailer for all brands we carry and only sell 100% genuine products with full manufacturer support.

Account & Membership

What are the benefits of creating an account?

Account holders enjoy faster checkout, order tracking, wish lists, exclusive deals, and access to our loyalty program with points for every purchase.

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email address. You'll receive a link to create a new password.

How do I update my account information?

Log into your account and click "Account Settings." From there you can update your personal information, shipping addresses, and communication preferences.

Corporate & Bulk Orders

Do you offer discounts for bulk orders?

Yes, we offer volume discounts for orders of 10+ units. Contact our corporate sales team for a customized quote based on your needs.

Can I set up a business account?

Absolutely. Business accounts receive net-30 terms, dedicated account management, and special pricing. Apply online or contact our sales team.

Do you offer purchase orders for government/education?

Yes, we accept government and educational purchase orders. Please register your organization with us to access special pricing and streamlined ordering.

What about tax-exempt purchases?

Tax-exempt organizations can submit their documentation through our portal. Once verified, tax will be automatically removed from your orders.
