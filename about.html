About TechGrid

Our Story

From Garage Startup to Tech Leader

Founded in 2015 by tech enthusiasts Sabih Uddin and Ben, TechGrid began as a small operation in a Karachi garage. What started as a passion project to bring cutting-edge technology to consumers has grown into one of the most trusted names in the tech retail space.

Today, we serve customers in over 30 countries with a curated selection of the latest gadgets, computers, and tech accessories. Our mission remains the same: to make innovative technology accessible to everyone while providing unparalleled customer service.

We carefully select every product in our inventory, working directly with manufacturers to ensure quality and value for our customers. Our team of tech experts tests and reviews each item, so you can shop with confidence.
